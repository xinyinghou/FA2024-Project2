Relax in a beautiful, bright private room in chic and calm condo in the vibrant Mission District, explore local outdoor dining restaurant scene. Located a block from shops, food, cafes & bars! Super comfortable mattress! Room has adjacent private bathroom just for guests. Easy access from the airport! *Super clean*
(This is not an entire private apartment)
Please read useful info in the House Rules section below (scroll all the way down and click on Show More), including check-in and parking details. A very sweet and quiet dog lives here. I am currently WFH a few days a week.

The apartment is a very cute and modern fully furnished apartment. The apartment is quite large, and will not feel cramped, unlike many San Francisco apartments. The entire apartment was fully gutted and remodeled recently.

Private bathroom (not an en-suite but located right next to your bedroom) is very clean, and is all yours :) I have a separate bathroom and do not share the bathroom.

A few additional things:

- Make yourself at home - I'm happy to host you :)

I live in a vibrant, popular and busy neighborhood, so there are more homeless people (they like to be where the crowds are), and hence more street trash. If you are uncomfortable with homelessness, this neighborhood won't be right for you. It is a very expensive and trendy neighborhood, but I don't control the homeless people, and would prefer not to have guests that ding me in the reviews because of the homeless situation all over SF.

- If you book this place, you acknowledge that you are savvy enough to follow detailed instructions to open lockbox for keys and retrieve keys. If you don't know what a lockbox is, this might be tough.
- Please let me know the day before your check-in a 30-60 minute window of your expected arrival time (just so I'm not walking out of the shower in a towel when you're walking in the door)

My place is within walking distance to great restaurants, bars, and diverse nightlife . I have tried to provide a varied list of the best our neighborhood has to offer in the welcome booklet when you arrive.

- If you bring a pet from prior approval from me - they must never be left alone unless they are crated inside and with prior approval from me.

I look forward to making your trip to San Francisco a memorable one!

PARKING
If you are driving, there is street parking in my neighborhood, and most of San Francisco. Its a little hit or miss but you can usually find something within a block or two of my place.

Street parking can be quick, or sometimes take 10 minutes - it completely varies every day. Its a great neighborhood with loads to do - I live near a bunch of restaurants and bars so sometimes everyone is out and about, and finding parking takes a while.

Just plan for it, and you won't be frustrated :)

The parking is allowed for people without residential stickers from 6pm to 8am the next day. ie. only 2 hour parking Monday-Saturday without a residential sticker.

So after 6pm on Saturday there is no restriction - so if you park after 6pm on Saturday, you should be good for the rest of the weekend :)

Please keep in mind that certain days have street cleaning! Please read the signs before you park.

There are several parking garages less than a 10 minute walk to my place if you prefer that - see below. I don't have a car and have never used any of them, but I have heard that they are safe and not terribly expensive.

660 Mission street garage, it's on 16th street and Mission (about a 7 minute walk from my place).

There is also overnight parking at El Capitan hotel on Mission between 19-20th street (very close to me)

GETTING AROUND
I uberpool or lyft line most places - but there are also several muni (bus) lines all around my building and should take you most places. BART is also super close and easily accessible.

AIRPORT:
If you are coming from the airport, try uber of lyft, but keep in mind that prices surcharge when it is busy, or raining.

You can always take the BART from SFO - the closest station is the 16th street station, but it can be a little dodgy at times, so getting off at the 24th street station is nicer, even though a slightly longer walk. I live on 19th street. Just walk up mission to get to my place. The blocks are long, and it's about a 7- 10 minute walk.

Uber/Lyft from SFO is about $50, or $30 if you UberPool/LyftLine. If you re 2 people traveling together, there is a marginal cost for the additional person on UberPool/LyftlLine (usually only $1 extra) - ie. $31 for 2 people.

Depending on the time or weather (rain), the prices can be lower, or higher, as is the case with Uber anytime. Prices vary based on demand.

BART is about $9/person. [Check website for actual price as it may change]. I have never taken BART from the airport, and just UberPool for convenience, but I believe it is straightforward.

Taxi is $50-$55, but previous guests have been charged up to $90 in traffic. I have never taken a taxi.

Super Shuttle - $27 for the two. You wait at the airport for other passengers then may or may not have stops before getting to the house.

NOTE: Please keep in mind that all ride-share programs require that you are picked up on the top level of the airport, but you collect your luggage on the lower level.
