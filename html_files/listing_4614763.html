    San Francisco, California, United States

    Walk to vibrant culture, nightlife & hip restaurants & cafes. The sunny side of the city! Combo Hipster-Latino-International neighborhood. Festive 24th St. is considered the San Francisco hotspot now. Some urban grittiness, similar to a little New York. Nearby is both cheap eats and fine dining from all over the world. Indy art galleries, bookshops galore, gourmet coffee shops, organic grocery stores, and so many little shops that specialize in their unique faire. Ground Central for festivals and start-ups. The most dynamic and ever-changing neighborhood in San Francisco. Blocks from Tech Gulch, SF General Hospital, BART. UCSF Benioff Mission Bay via free shuttle a block away.
