Modern separate suite (~450 sq ft) in the colorful Mission district. Separate entrance, en-suite bathroom, mini fridge and microwave. Steps from Mexican food, sushi, bookstores, murals & several bus lines. 10 min walk to BART.

The space
The private guest suite is about 450 sq ft on the ground floor of our home. The space is clean, has good natural light, wood floors and its own private bathroom. The room is furnished with a queen size bed and therefore is perfect for 1 or 2 guests. However, upon request for those traveling with a child, we can provide a twin size Aerobed and possibly a pack and play crib. (Note there is an extra guest fee.)

The suite has a flat screen television and wifi access. There is also a small table for casual eating and those who need to get some laptop time in.

While the San Francisco Shelter in Place was in effect, we took the opportunity to improve our backyard! Back there you will find our shed, Michelle’s succulent collection, and a place to sit and enjoy the sunshine. You may run into us tinkering with things back there, but you are welcome to enjoy the space with us.

Our home is located in the vibrant and fun Mission district. The space is perfect for visiting grandparents, travelers, visiting professionals and people who are visiting patients at SF General which is only a couple blocks away. There are several different bus lines within a few block radius of our house and we are less than 10 minutes walking distance from the 24th and Mission BART station. For great shopping and restaurants offering almost any cuisine you could ask for, walk about 15 minutes to the Valencia Street corridor.

San Francisco, California, United States

The Mission is a fun and vibrant neighborhood - there is so much to do and see here! We see eating tours and mural viewing tours in and around our neighborhood all the time. A few times a year, there are parades that go down 24th Street, such as for Dia de los Muertos and Cinco de Mayo.

We are just a few minutes walk from some of the city's most popular restaurants and food establishments including: Flour + Water, Dynamo Donuts, Humphrey Slocombe (unique ice cream), Blowfish Sushi and Universal Cafe. Visit Charles Chocolates for a tour! Heath Ceramics and Tartine Manufactory are also nearby.

The Mission has one of the warmest microclimates in the city, so we get a lot of sun and less fog than other areas.
