Large, light filled, quiet, private 2 bedroom apartment/guest suite with split bathroom (commode and sink in one room, bath/shower and sink in other), kitchenette, and ceilings up to 16'. We have been working on this space for the last 2 years to get the details just right, including hand built furniture and art by local artists. Above the din of the Mission, this apartment is a unique space built in the attic of a large Edwardian house run by artists Laurel Roth Hope and Andy Diaz Hope .

The space
This guest suite is a unique custom space perfect for a relaxing escape or productive work trip in San Francisco. No detail was spared--all of the doors and architectural details were loving restored from houses of the period of our building (the 1890's). Most of the furniture was custom built by us (Laurel and Andy) using the salvaged structural Douglas Fir removed during the construction process. Light fixtures are a mix of restored early 1900's and modern. The space was created to augment our guests' stay and encourage relaxation rather than parties. We have created special areas for reading and relaxing throughout the space. The light is incredible and space is very quiet but centrally located in the Mission near several award winning restaurants and local shops and eateries. From the apartment you have 360° views of San Francisco!

For your safety during the Covid 19 pandemic, we want to highlight some specifics of the space for your (and our) enhanced safety:

• Large Blueair Pure 121 air purifier with HEPA filter in apartment
• Large operable windows and skylights throughout space for rapid air circulation
• AirBnB enhanced cleaning protocols observed
• Shoe free space

Where you’ll be

San Francisco, California, United States

The Mission is a popular, vibrant neighborhood full of families, artists, locally owned shops, art spaces, and bookstores. We're within blocks of great eating in every direction--Atlas Cafe, Bon, Nene; Tartine, El Metate, Flour and Water, San Jalisco and many other great restaurants, plus walking distance from Mission or Valencia Streets for shopping or nightlife. The Heath ceramics factory and store is a few blocks away. There's a rec center half a block away with gym facilities, Mission Cliffs climbing gym down the street, and a rent by the hour Bay Wheels bike kiosk on our block. The Mission is the sunniest neighborhood in SF!
