A charming space in a great location, tucked away from the street, surrounded by trees and flowers offers Comfort & Culture. Your own very private space with separate entrance and patio not connected to the house upstairs. Unfortunately the neighbor is doing a big renovation project that might cause noise. Working hours are 8am to 4pm. Best to visit on weekends, of course, or if you are busy during the day away from the house.

The space
The space is small and cosy with a big desk and a big comfy bed with spring mattress. A French door opens to your own private patio with table and chairs, ideal for an outdoor snack or evening drink. Between bedroom and kitchen area is a 6ft high beam - so tall people be aware! There is no full kitchen, only microwave, small fridge, hot water, Keurig coffee machine, toaster and a double hotplate - no stove and no sink in the kitchen area just in the bathroom next to it.
